## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art portrait bust** of a **very cute 3-year-old
boy** for a visual-novel dialogue box. **512 x 512.** Head and upper
shoulders, **facing slightly toward the viewer's left** (toward the mom
portrait opposite). **Big bright joyful expression** — wide eyes, open
little smile, like he's just been reunited with his mom.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Cute
chubby toddler proportions (large round head, small shoulders).

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Soft blond hair** in a fluffy little kid cut.
- **Big brown eyes**, rosy cheeks, tiny dimpled grin.
- **Purple t-shirt** visible at the bottom of the bust.

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white, NOT a pastel. The user will manually cut out the
background. **Give Zeno a clear darker outline** so the white can be
removed cleanly.

**Text:** a tiny graphic on the t-shirt (small dinosaur, star, "Z") is
welcome. **No dialogue bubbles or UI labels.**

Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
