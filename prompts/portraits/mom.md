# Mom — dialogue portrait

- **Save to:** `public/assets/portraits/mom_portrait.png`
- **Size:** 512 x 512 PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `DialogueOverlay` (car cutscene + classroom cutscenes).

> 📎 **Attach a reference photo of mom to the AI tool** when running this
> prompt. The description below should match the reference; pixel-art
> stylisation is expected, but the hair, eyes, and overall face should
> clearly resemble the photo. Also match the appearance used in
> `characters/mom.md`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art portrait bust** of a **loving mother in her
30s** for a visual-novel dialogue box. **512 x 512.** Head and upper
shoulders, **facing slightly toward the viewer's right** (talking to
someone on the right). Warm friendly expression, soft confident smile.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading.

**Appearance (must match):**
- **Long natural red hair** (warm auburn / copper red, not orange-cartoon
  red, not strawberry blonde) flowing past her shoulders, partly visible
  framing her face.
- Fair skin.
- **Pink sweater** (soft warm pink knit) visible at the bottom of the
  bust.
- Optional: small earrings or thin necklace.

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white, NOT a pastel. The user will manually cut out the
background. **Give the portrait a clear darker outline** so the white can
be removed cleanly even where she has fair skin or white clothing.

**Text:** none. **No dialogue bubbles, name tags, or UI labels** — drawn
by the engine.

Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
