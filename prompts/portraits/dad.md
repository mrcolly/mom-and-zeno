# Dad — dialogue portrait

- **Save to:** `public/assets/portraits/dad_portrait.png`
- **Size:** 512 x 512 PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `DialogueOverlay` (car cutscene phone call). Dad never
  appears as a body sprite — only this portrait.

> 📎 **Attach a reference photo of dad to the AI tool** when running this
> prompt. The description below should match the reference; pixel-art
> stylisation is expected, but the hair, beard, eyes, and overall face
> should clearly resemble the photo.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art portrait bust** of a **kind dad in his 30s**
for a visual-novel dialogue box during a phone call. **512 x 512.** Head
and upper shoulders, **facing slightly toward the viewer's left** (so he
visually faces the mom portrait opposite). Calm supportive expression,
slight reassuring smile.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading.

**Appearance (must match the reference photo):**
- **Medium-length brown hair.**
- **Small / slim frame**, slender face — not bulky.
- **Medium-length brown beard** (well-groomed, not bushy, matching the
  hair color).
- **Brown eyes.**
- Fair skin.
- A simple casual top (plain shirt or sweater).
- No glasses, no hat, no phone in frame.

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white, NOT a pastel. The user will manually cut out the
background. **Give the portrait a clear darker outline** so the white can
be removed cleanly even where he has fair skin or white clothing.

**Text:** none. **No dialogue bubbles, name tags, or UI labels** — drawn
by the engine.

Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
