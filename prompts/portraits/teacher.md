## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art portrait bust** of a **kindergarten teacher**
for a visual-novel dialogue box. **512 x 512.** Head and upper shoulders,
**facing slightly toward the viewer's left** (toward the mom portrait
opposite). Polite patient expression, small reserved smile.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Subdued
grey / soft brown / muted white palette.

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Very pale skin** (almost porcelain).
- **Shoulder-length straight brown hair**, parted to one side.
- **Small round or rectangular glasses**, thin dark frames.
- **Plain grey crewneck sweater over white collared shirt**, no flashy
  jewelry.

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white, NOT a pastel. The user will manually cut out the
background. **Give the teacher a clear darker outline** so the white can
be removed cleanly — important here, since her skin is very pale
(porcelain) and her shirt is white.

**Text:** a small **"MISS" name tag** on her sweater is welcome. **No
dialogue bubbles or UI labels.**

Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
