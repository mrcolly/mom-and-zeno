# Obstacle — shoe

- **Save to:** `public/assets/obstacles/shoe.png` (new folder)
- **Size:** 96 x 96, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of a **single child's sneaker
lying on the floor**. **96 x 96, PNG on a solid pure white background.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Bright
primary color upper with white sole.

**Must include:**
- Small kid-sized low-top sneaker, side profile, laces facing up.
- Bright **red, yellow, or green** upper, white sole, white laces tied in
  a cute bow, small white toe cap.
- **Sits at the bottom of the canvas — sole aligned to floor. No shadow.**

**Text:** small invented brand logo on the side is fine. **No game UI
labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
shoe a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly even
where the shoe itself is white (sole, laces, toe cap). No border, no
watermark.
