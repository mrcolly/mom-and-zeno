# Obstacle — small bicycle

- **Save to:** `public/assets/obstacles/small_bicycle.png` (new folder)
- **Size:** 96 x 96, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of a **small kid's bicycle with
training wheels, standing on the floor**. **96 x 96, PNG on a solid pure
white background.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Bright
friendly frame color.

**Must include:**
- Tiny low-frame bike with **two main wheels + two small training wheels
  at the back** (clearly visible).
- Bright **sky blue, mint green, or hot pink** frame; white or red saddle.
- Straight handlebars with colored grips and short streamers; a small
  chrome bell.
- White tires; spokes suggested as a simple X-pattern (don't try to render
  every spoke).
- Optional: small contrasting basket on the front.
- **All four wheels touch the floor baseline at the bottom of the canvas.
  No shadow.**

**Text:** a small sticker / decal on the frame (heart, star, "KIDDO") is
fine. **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
bike a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly even
where the bike itself is white (tires, saddle). No border, no watermark.
