# Obstacle — small newborn

- **Save to:** `public/assets/obstacles/newborn.png` (new folder)
- **Size:** 96 x 96, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene` — yes, mom is jumping over a baby. The game is
  silly on purpose.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of a **tiny newborn baby
swaddled in a soft blanket, lying calmly on the floor**. **96 x 96, PNG
on a solid pure white background.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Pastel
yellow or pale blue blanket. Cute, wholesome — **the baby looks safe and
serene, NOT in distress.**

**Must include:**
- Fully wrapped in a pastel blanket, only the round little head and a
  tiny tuft of hair sticking out at the top.
- Eyes peacefully closed (small horizontal lines), rosy cheeks, calm
  sleeping smile.
- **Lies at the bottom of the canvas — swaddle base aligned to floor. No
  shadow.**

**Text:** a tiny embroidered "BABY" or "Z" on the blanket is sweet.
**No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
baby + blanket a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed
cleanly. No border, no watermark.
