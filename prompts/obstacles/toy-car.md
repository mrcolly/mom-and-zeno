# Obstacle — toy car

- **Save to:** `public/assets/obstacles/toy_car.png` (new folder)
- **Size:** 96 x 96, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of a **chunky kid's toy car on
the floor**. **96 x 96, PNG on a solid pure white background.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Bright
primary colors. Cartoonish, Pixar-Cars feel — short body, oversized
wheels, big friendly headlight.

**Must include:**
- Bright primary body (e.g. **fire-engine red** with yellow accents, or
  sunshine yellow with red).
- Round black-and-grey wheels with hubcaps.
- Tiny grin painted on the front. No real-brand markings.
- **Sits at the bottom of the canvas — wheels touching floor baseline.
  No shadow.**

**Text:** a small invented brand or number on the door (e.g. "ZOOM!" or
"42") is fine. **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
toy car a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly. No
border, no watermark.
