- **Save to:** `public/assets/obstacles/teddy_bear.png` (new folder)
- **Size:** 96 x 96, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of a **small plush teddy bear
sitting on the floor**. **96 x 96, PNG on a solid pure white background.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered fur shading. Soft
caramel-brown / cream palette. Cute, kid-friendly.

**Must include:**
- Caramel-brown fur, lighter tan belly and snout, round head.
- Small black bead eyes, tiny stitched smile, rounded ears with pink inner.
- Sitting upright, legs splayed forward, stubby arms hanging.
- Optional: small red ribbon bow at the neck.
- **Sits at the bottom of the canvas — base aligned to floor. No shadow.**

**Text:** none expected. **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
teddy a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly. No
border, no watermark.
