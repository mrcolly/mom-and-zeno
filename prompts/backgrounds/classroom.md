# Kindergarten classroom (interior)

- **Save to:** `public/assets/backgrounds/classroom.png`
- **Size:** 1280 x 720, opaque PNG
- **Used by:** `KindergartenCutsceneScene` — static scene, mom + teacher +
  Zeno stand on top of it.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of the **inside of a kindergarten
classroom**. **1280 x 720, opaque PNG, single static scene** (no tiling
required).

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. **Walls
in the same warm yellow** as the entrance corridor (so it reads as the
same building). Side-on parallel projection.

**Critical constraints:**
- **NO left or right side walls.** The back wall, ceiling, and floor must
  run flush off-screen on both edges so the teacher can walk off the right
  side during the cutscene to fetch Zeno. No corner column, doorway, or
  vertical wall on either edge.
- **Leave a clear horizontal "stage" strip across the floor** where mom
  (left third) and teacher + Zeno (right third) will stand. Don't put tall
  furniture in those zones.

**Feature element — row of small lockers along the back wall:**
A long horizontal row of child-sized cubbies, each with a **pastel-colored
door** (alternating mint, sky blue, peach, lilac, butter yellow, coral
pink), small round handle, and a **clearly readable name plate at the top
of each door**. A few doors slightly ajar with backpacks / coats peeking
out. Coat hooks below the row.

**Required names** on the lockers (all caps reads best at pixel scale):
**ZENO, NATHAN, ALESSANDRO, GINEVRA, RICCARDO, THEO**, plus fillers like
**MIA, LEO, SOFIA, MATTEO, ELENA**. **ZENO's locker must be clearly
visible** somewhere it isn't covered by the standing characters — give it
a standout door color (e.g. mint or sky blue).

**Other decoration (light touch):** a pendant light or two on the ceiling,
a tall window in a top corner with afternoon light spilling in (kept away
from the very edge), an alphabet poster, a children's-art bulletin board,
a small bookshelf, optionally a circular rainbow rug. Two or three low
child-sized tables / chairs in the foreground placed away from the
character "stage" zones.

**Text:** locker name plates as listed above. Other in-scene text
encouraged ("A B C D" poster, "WELCOME", book titles, "MY FAMILY"
drawing). **No game UI text** ("PLAY", "START", score, etc.).

**No characters, no UI overlays.** Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
