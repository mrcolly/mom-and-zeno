# Race corridor background (yellow building entrance hallway)

- **Save to:** `public/assets/backgrounds/school_interior.png`
- **Size:** 1280 x 720, opaque PNG
- **Used by:** `RaceScene` — tiled ~6 times across a 7680 px track.
- **Tileable horizontally:** **YES — critical, repeated 6 times.**

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view of the **entrance hallway of an
apartment / community building** — the public area parents have to run
through to reach the kindergarten door at the far end. **Not inside the
kindergarten itself, no kid space.** **1280 x 720, opaque PNG, MUST tile
seamlessly horizontally** (leftmost and rightmost pixel columns identical,
no visible seam when repeated).

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. **Walls
painted bright cheerful yellow** (warm mustard / amber). Side-on parallel
projection — no perspective lines.

**Composition (top to bottom):**
- Plain cream ceiling — no fixtures, just a thin trim line where it meets
  the wall.
- Yellow wall band, **modestly decorated, not cluttered**. The same tile
  is repeated 6 times during the race, so keep things sparse and rhythmic.
  Pick **a small handful (~3-4 elements per tile)** from this list and
  arrange them with **generous empty wall space between them**, well away
  from both the left and right edges so they don't bunch at seams:
  - a **wall-mounted intercom / buzzer panel**
  - a **small framed notice or building announcement**
  - a **tall thin radiator** inset against the baseboard
  - a **simple wall sconce or small wall lamp**
  - a **small painting or photo frame** in muted colors
  - a **potted plant** on the floor against the wall
  Pick a different mix than just one of each — for example: intercom +
  radiator + small framed notice + a single sconce. **No mailbox bank,
  no bulletin board, nothing childlike** (no children's drawings, no
  alphabet posters, no coat hooks).
- Light wood / pale linoleum / polished tile floor at the bottom (~140 px)
  with a thin baseboard line.

**Text:** if you include a notice or sign, keep it short and legible
(e.g. "FLOOR 1", "EXIT", a small unit-number panel). **No game UI text**
("RUN", "JUMP", score, etc.).

**No characters. No door at the end** — the finish-line door is a
separate sprite drawn on top. **No tall foreground furniture** that would
clip running characters. Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
