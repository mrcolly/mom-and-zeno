# Mother's Day splash background

- **Save to:** `public/assets/backgrounds/splash.png`
- **Size:** 1280 x 720, opaque PNG
- **Used by:** `MenuScene` (title screen) and `HappyMothersDayScene` (final
  splash).

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** celebratory background for a Mother's Day
splash screen. **1280 x 720, opaque PNG, single static scene.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. **Soft
pastel palette** — coral pink at the bottom blending up to peach and
lavender. Wholesome, gift-like mood. Flat side-on composition.

**Composition:**
- Soft pastel sky gradient.
- Floating pixel **hearts of varying sizes** scattered across the image,
  with a few small stars and tiny pixel flowers.
- **Keep the center of the image visually quiet** — the engine prints the
  game title and buttons over the center at runtime; that area must stay
  readable. Push decorations toward the edges and corners.

**Text:** small decorative confetti accents are fine — tiny "♥", a ribbon
banner reading "MOM" tucked into a corner, a flower with a tag, etc. **Do
NOT render "Happy Mother's Day", "Mom & Zeno", "PLAY AGAIN" or any other
game-engine strings** — those are drawn by the engine and would duplicate.

**No characters, no buildings.** Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
