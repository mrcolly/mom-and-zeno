# Race finish: open kindergarten door with teacher peeking out

- **Save to:** `public/assets/backgrounds/finish_door.png` (new file)
- **Size:** 480 x 640, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene` — placed at the finish line. The user will
  composite it onto the corridor in Photoshop, so this prompt only needs
  to produce a clean isolated door asset.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** sprite of an **open kindergarten classroom
door, with the teacher peeking out**. **480 x 640, PNG on a solid pure
white background.** The door asset will be composited onto a yellow
corridor background separately.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Warm
cohesive palette matching the rest of the game.

**CRITICAL — show the FULL door:**
- The **entire door must be fully visible from top to bottom and left to
  right**, including the **complete frame, the full door panel, the floor
  threshold at the bottom, and the sign above at the top**. Nothing
  should be cropped or cut off by the canvas edges.
- Leave a small margin of plain white space around the door so it clearly
  reads as a complete, isolated asset — not a zoomed-in detail shot.

**Must include:**
- A wide wooden door **swung open**, clearly readable as "this door is
  open" (door panel visible attached to one side of the frame). The
  **entire door panel** must be visible — not just the top half, not just
  the opening.
- Through the opening: a **warm glow** + a brief glimpse of the
  **kindergarten classroom interior** (a colorful poster, a hint of
  cubbies / children's-art splash) — kid-space inside.
- A small sign mounted above the door clearly reading **"ZENO'S CLASS"**
  (or just "ZENO" in big friendly block letters with a heart).
- The **teacher** standing just inside the doorway, leaning out to look
  out. Visible from the chest up. **Very pale skin, small glasses,
  shoulder-length straight brown hair, plain grey sweater**, calm
  reserved expression with a small patient smile.

**Text:** the **"ZENO'S CLASS"** sign is required. Small in-scene labels
on posters glimpsed inside are fine. **No game UI text** ("FINISH",
"GOAL", arrows, etc.).

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. Only the door + frame + sign + interior glimpse + teacher
should be drawn; everything else is plain white. The user will manually
cut out the background. **Give the door, frame, and teacher clear darker
outlines** so the white can be removed cleanly even where the teacher's
shirt is white and her skin is very pale. No surrounding hallway wall,
no floor strip, no shadow. Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
