# Street background (car cutscene)

- **Save to:** `public/assets/backgrounds/street.png`
- **Size:** 1280 x 720, opaque PNG
- **Used by:** `CarCutsceneScene` — the car drives left to right across this.
- **Tileable horizontally:** yes.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view background of a quiet suburban
street at late afternoon. **1280 x 720, opaque PNG, must tile seamlessly
horizontally** (leftmost and rightmost pixel columns identical).

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Warm
cohesive palette (golden-hour orange sky, soft pinks, dusty blues). Side-on
parallel projection — no perspective lines.

**Composition:**
- Sky in upper third with a couple of pixel clouds.
- Row of small suburban houses + a corner shop in the middle band.
- Sidewalk and grass tufts at the bottom.
- **Leave a flat dark-asphalt horizontal strip ~120 px tall around y=550** —
  the engine draws the road and lane dashes on top.

**Text:** small in-scene signs (e.g. "BAKERY", "ONE WAY") are welcome,
sparse and tile-aligned. **No game UI text** ("PLAY", "RUN", etc.).

**No characters, no cars.** Single PNG, no border, no watermark.
