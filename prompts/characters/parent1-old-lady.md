# Parent 1 — old lady (slowest rival)

- **Save to:** `public/assets/characters/parent1.png`
- **Size:** 96 x 192, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene` — visually the slowest rival.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view sprite of an **elderly
grandmother**. **96 x 192, PNG on a solid pure white background.**
Standing pose, **facing right**, **leaning forward on a wooden cane**
(right hand), hurrying as fast as she can.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Muted
lilac / dusty rose / soft beige palette.

**Must read at a glance:**
- White-grey hair in a small bun, deep wrinkles, slight hunch.
- Long modest floral skirt + soft pastel cardigan + flat orthopedic shoes.
- **Wooden cane** clearly visible.
- Small handbag on her left forearm; round glasses on a chain optional.
- Kind but determined expression.

**Text:** none expected. **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
character (and her cane) a clear darker outline** so the white can be
removed cleanly even where she has white parts (white-grey hair, white
collar). No border, no watermark.
