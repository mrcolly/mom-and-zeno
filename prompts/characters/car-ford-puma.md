# Car — grey Ford Puma

- **Save to:** `public/assets/characters/car.png`
- **Size:** 320 x 160, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `CarCutsceneScene` — drives left to right across the street.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side profile of a **grey Ford Puma
crossover SUV** (the modern 2020s model — tall front, short bonnet, small
upright rear hatch — NOT the 90s coupe). **320 x 160, PNG on a solid
pure white background.** **Front of the car points to the right.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Side-on
parallel projection — no perspective.

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Medium metallic grey body** ("Magnetic Grey" / gunmetal) with darker
  dithered shading on the lower body.
- Distinct Puma silhouette: tall front, raised hood, sloping roofline
  dropping toward the rear hatch, prominent front wheel arch.
- Two side doors visible (driver + rear passenger), silver door handles.
- **Tinted side windows with mom's silhouette in the driver's seat** as a
  small dark shape behind the front window.
- Black tires with grey 5-spoke alloy rims, fully circular, fully visible.
- Front: angular headlight + small **blue Ford oval badge** on the grille.
- Rear: angular taillight, small **license plate** with a short readable
  code (e.g. "ZN-MOM-1").

**Text:** the "Ford" badge and license plate are welcome and should be
legible. **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give
the car a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly. No
motion lines, no dust trails, no damage. Single PNG, no border, no
watermark.
