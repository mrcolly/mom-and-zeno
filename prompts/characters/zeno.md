# Zeno (the son)

- **Save to:** `public/assets/characters/zeno.png`
- **Size:** 80 x 160, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand). Smaller than adults — he's 3.
- **Used by:** `KindergartenCutsceneScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view sprite of a **very cute
3-year-old boy**. **80 x 160, PNG on a solid pure white background.**
Standing pose, **facing right**, with a little bounce — like he just
spotted his mom. **Big bright happy expression.**

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Cute
chubby toddler proportions (large round head, small body, short arms).
Wholesome, warm-cohesive palette.

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Soft blond hair** in a fluffy little kid cut.
- **Big brown eyes**, rosy cheeks, tiny dimpled smile.
- **Purple t-shirt** (warm, slightly violet — not too dark).
- **Black pants** (cotton trousers).
- **Bright green sneakers** with white laces.

**Text:** a tiny graphic on the t-shirt (small dinosaur, star, "Z") is
welcome. **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give
Zeno a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly even
where he has white parts (white shoelaces). No border, no watermark.
