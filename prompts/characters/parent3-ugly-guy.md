# Parent 3 — ugly guy with a cigarette

- **Save to:** `public/assets/characters/parent3.png`
- **Size:** 96 x 192, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view sprite of an **a-little-run-down
middle-aged man trying way too hard to look cool**. **96 x 192, PNG on a
solid pure white background.** Standing pose mid-stride, **facing right**,
slightly slouched. Comedic vain character — silly, not menacing. **Not
gross, not smelly, not stained** — just a bit of a faded look.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Muted
warm palette (faded olive / dusty beige / soft brown).

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Mostly bald with a few sad wisps of hair** on the sides.
- **Big prominent nose** — the silhouette feature, clearly visible in
  side profile.
- **Sunglasses** (chunky pixel-art shades — dark lenses, simple frame).
- **Slight pot belly, slightly slouched posture.**
- **Polo / collared shirt with the COLLAR POPPED UP** — the raised
  collar should be unmistakable, framing the back of his neck. Pick a
  faded color (washed olive, dusty beige, muted maroon).
- Plain trousers / chinos in a neutral tone, simple loafers or sneakers.
  Clean, not tattered.
- **Lit cigarette in his right hand** with a tiny orange ember and a
  thin curl of grey smoke rising from it.

**Text:** small natural detail OK (a tiny faded brand logo on the polo).
**No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
character (and the cigarette + smoke wisp) a clear darker outline** so
the white can be removed cleanly. No border, no watermark.
