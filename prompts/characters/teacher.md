# Teacher

- **Save to:** `public/assets/characters/teacher.png`
- **Size:** 96 x 192, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `KindergartenCutsceneScene` (also referenced for the
  finish-line door sprite).

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view sprite of a **kindergarten
teacher**. **96 x 192, PNG on a solid pure white background.** Standing
pose, **facing right**, calm and patient (hands clasped at her waist or
holding a small notebook).

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Subdued
grey / soft brown / muted white palette. Side-on parallel projection.

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Very pale skin** (almost porcelain).
- **Shoulder-length straight brown hair**, parted to one side.
- **Small round or rectangular glasses** with thin dark frames.
- **Plain grey crewneck sweater over a white collared shirt**, knee-length
  grey skirt or plain dark trousers, neutral flat shoes.
- Reserved bland-but-pleasant expression.

**Text:** a tiny **name tag reading "MISS"** on her sweater is welcome.
**No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
teacher a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly
even though her shirt is white and her skin is very pale (porcelain). No
border, no watermark.
