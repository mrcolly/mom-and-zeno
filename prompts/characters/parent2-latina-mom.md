## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view sprite of a **Latina mother in
her early 30s**. **96 x 192, PNG on a solid pure white background.**
Standing pose mid-stride, **facing right**, leaning forward like she's
running. Confident focused expression with a slight smirk.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading. Warm
terracotta / coral / maroon palette.

**Must read at a glance:**
- **Medium-length straight black hair** to just below the chin.
- Warm tan skin.
- **Slim build with soft curves around hips/waist** (a little fat, not
  thin-thin).
- Fitted long-sleeve top in a warm tone, dark fitted trousers / leggings,
  white sneakers.
- Small gold hoop earrings, small crossbody bag bouncing at her hip.

**Text:** small natural detail OK (sneaker logo, bag slogan). **No game UI
labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
character a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly
even where she has white parts (white sneakers). No border, no watermark.
