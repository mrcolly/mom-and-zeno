# Mom (player character)

- **Save to:** `public/assets/characters/mom.png`
- **Size:** 96 x 192, PNG with **plain white background** (will be cut out by hand)
- **Used by:** `RaceScene`, `KindergartenCutsceneScene`.

## Prompt

A 2D **16-bit SNES pixel-art** side-view sprite of a **loving mother in
her 30s**. **96 x 192, PNG on a solid pure white background.**
Single standing pose, **facing right**, mid-stride, friendly determined
expression.

**Style:** crisp pixel edges, no antialiasing, dithered shading, chunky
outlines. Side-on parallel projection.

**Appearance (must match):**
- **Long natural red hair** (warm auburn / copper red, not orange-cartoon
  red, not strawberry blonde) flowing past her shoulders.
- Fair skin.
- **Pink sweater** (soft warm pink, comfortable knit).
- **Black pants** (plain trousers).
- **Blue shoes** (flat, comfortable, something she can run in).
- **No belt.**
- **No bag**, no purse, no backpack, no accessories at the waist or
  shoulders.

**Text:** small natural detail OK (tiny shirt logo, tote slogan, name
tag). **No game UI labels.**

**Background: solid pure white (#FFFFFF)** — flat and uniform across the
whole image. **NOT a checkered transparency pattern**, NOT a gradient,
NOT off-white. The user will manually cut out the background. **Give the
character a clear darker outline** so the white can be removed cleanly
even where the character has white parts. No border, no watermark.
